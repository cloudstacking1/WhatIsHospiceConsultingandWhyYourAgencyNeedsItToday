Understanding Hospice Care

Hospice care focuses on providing comfort and support to patients with life-limiting illnesses, prioritizing quality of life over curative treatments. Services typically include pain and symptom management, emotional and spiritual support, and assistance for families during challenging times. The goal is to address the holistic needs of patients and their loved ones, ensuring dignity and comfort in their final days.?
